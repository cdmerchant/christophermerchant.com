====== French Onion Dip ======

Executed 9 December 2018; WIP

==== Ingredients ====

  * 2 medium onions, sliced thinly
  * 2 cloves garlic
  * 1c sour cream
  * 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  * parsley/salt/pepper to taste

==== Directions ====

  - Caramelize onions over med/low heat until deep brown, deglazing with water as needed
  - Place all ingredients into food processor and mix until uniform
