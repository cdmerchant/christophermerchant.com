====== roasted mushrooms ======

Executed 10 12 18 - WIP

==== Ingredients ====

  *  2lb mushrooms, washed/trimmed/quartered
  * 1/4c extra virgin olive oil
  * 4-5 sprigs thyme or rosemary
  * 2 tbsp chopped parsley/chives or other fresh herbs

==== Directions ====

  - Preheat oven to 350F and toss mushrooms with olive oil, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  - Transfer mushrooms to foil-lined baking vessel in an even layer, spreading thyme and rosemary on top of mushrooms. Move to oven for 15 minutes.
  - Drain liquid into a separate container (great sauce, very flavorful) and reserve.
  - Return mushrooms to oven for 30 minutes longer, discarding thyme sprigs and toss with parsley/chives/tarragon, serve immediately.
